FAQs and RSVP

Will there be a ceremony on November, 18th?

Nope! Thor and Jordan will be eloping in Savannah the weekend before.

Can I bring a guest or children?

Yes! We'd love for you to bring your partner even if they were not listed on the invitation. Just let us know when you RSVP! You're also welcome to bring your children. Up to you if you want a night away from them!

What should I wear?

The dress code is semi-formal. Jordan will still wear her wedding dress, so please refrain from wearing white. There is also no wedding party, so you don't need to worry about accidentally matching any bridesmaids' dresses.

Where should I park?

There is no parking at SCAD Ivy Hall. We strongly encourage using a ride share app to drop you off directly at the venue. If you choose to drive, we will have parking available at the Yaarab Shriners Temple. (400 Ponce De Leon Ave NE) A shuttle will be looping between parking and the venue during our reception.

Where do I enter?

When you arrive, there will be a gate at the entrance on Piedmont Ave. Please go through the gate and take the path up to the venue. Please refrain from walking up the driveway... the cobblestone is treacherous!

What are we eating?

We've reached out to many of our favorite local spots and can't wait for you to try them! Instead of a seated dinner, you can choose to eat whenever you want! Outside, we will have Tres Jardines serving tacos and sides from their truck and Tony's will be serving Italian Beef Sandwiches and Chicago Dogs. A variety of desserts from Little Tart Bakery will be inside as well as an open bar. If you have any food allergies, please let us know!
